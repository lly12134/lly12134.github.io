About the women’s volleyball game

As early as the 2016 Rio Olympics, I was bored to watch TV, and I happened to see the first game of the Chinese women’s volleyball team. I remember very clearly that it was against the Dutch team, and the Chinese women’s volleyball team was completely defeated, and I didn’t understand the rules of the volleyball game at that time, I only remember that the adults shook their heads again and again. said that the Chinese women’s volleyball team is no longer good. But a few days later, I turned on the TV again, and it happened to be the live broadcast of the women’s volleyball final, but there was a figure of the Chinese women’s volleyball team. I vaguely remember that my mother said before the start of the game that the Chinese women’s volleyball team should be difficult to win, and how the Chinese women’s volleyball team should play with Serbia’s fierce and masculine style of play. But I don’t know why I inexplicably believe that the Chinese team can win, and the result is the same, the Chinese women’s volleyball team finally won the Olympic championship again after many years. At this point, I experienced the charm of competitive sports with an inexplicable sense of happiness in my heart, and I fell in love with watching volleyball games ever since.
After that, I watched a lot of games, big and small. Getting to know the different players makes you even more immersed in the charm of volleyball, because it is not a one-person game, but a team sport in which various positions work together. The secondary attack of the iron wall, the main attack of the powerful smash, the strong breakthrough that shocked the four, the wonderful pass of the second setter, and the quick response of the free man all reflect the charm of this game.
After graduating from the college entrance examination, I finally had the opportunity to watch the first live women’s volleyball game in my life. The World Women’s Volleyball League held in Hong Kong can only be said to be completely different from what you see on the cold screen, and it is indescribably touching.
In 2024, the World Women’s Volleyball League was still held in Hong Kong and Macau, so I naturally did not miss this opportunity to come to Macau to watch the game. It’s so memorable again.And luckily, the reserved seat right in front of me, and the players were sitting in front of me. They gladly agreed to take a photo with them. It’s amazing to see the people who used to be on the screen so kind in front of you.
And there are countless wonderful and glorious moments in competitive sports, which will also accompany these countless sad endings. With the passage of time, there will inevitably be a replacement of the old and the new, and it is also nostalgic and sad to watch familiar players retire one by one. And for those athletes who have strong personal ability, but because the strength of other people in the national team is not good, it is also regrettable. Like Lonneke Sloetjes from the Netherlands, she was a strong response that had a strong impact on me since my time in Rio. The Dutch team also won the highest result in the history of the Dutch women’s volleyball team at that time. However, the state of the players in the team declined seriously, the results of the whole team were not satisfactory, and she retired after a few years. Thinking of a sentence, how can it not be regrettable that the best awards in each event are full, but there is no medal in the three major competitions.
Another player from Serbia is Tijana Bošković, who is my favorite player. From Rio to the present, watching her mature step by step from a genius girl, to a complete world superstar with comprehensive skills, one side of the other, how can you not like it. At the 2024 Paris Olympics, although there was a game at 3 o’clock in the morning, I was still willing to stay up late to watch the game for her, but what I saw was that she was the only attacking point in the whole team, and other attackers could not help her share the offensive pressure at all. As a result, she scored 39 points alone in one game, and the others combined for only 35 points. She can’t win such a game. The talented girl was only 17 years old when she debuted, and now she is 27 years old, how many four years can an athlete have? Watching her miss the Olympic gold medal again, how can she not feel distressed when she sits lonely on the side after the game? “I don’t want to have anything to do with the national team, I just want you to have no regrets” is that I really hope she can win the gold medal, because she deserves it, but volleyball is never a one-man game. Therefore, after struggling and fighting, not only exciting success, but also regret is always the charm of competitive sports.
